FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

SHIPPING

How long does shipping take?

Usually you will receive your jacket within 2-3 days.

How much faster is express shipping?

Express shipping refers to orders that will be shipped and recieved within 24 hours. This option is only available for Norwegian orders.

Where can I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number can be found either on your profile, under "purchases", or in the confirmation email which you recieved when ordering!

How can I get free shipping?

All orders above £ 130 ensures free shipping.

RETURNS

How do I make a return?

In your package you get a return slip which you could use to return the products. Otherwise e-mail us to get a new slip!

Where can I find my return slip?

The return slip will be shipped in the package containing the products.

How much does it cost to make a return?

Returns on orders under £ 130 will cost £ 8. Orders above £ 130 gets free returns.

When do I get my money back after returning an item?

The money should be back on your account within 2-3 days after we've recieved the package.

PAYMENT

Is it possible to pay with credit card?

All orders above £ 130 ensures free shipping.

Where do I use my giftcard?

You can use your giftcard in one of the forms on the payment-page.

My coupon code does not work.

If your coupon code does not work, please check if the code have expired or been used before.

Is it possible to pay with Vipps?

Unfortunately we can't do payments through Vipps just yet, but this alternative coming soon!
